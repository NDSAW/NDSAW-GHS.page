About NDSAW

The National Digital Security & Analysis Wing (NDSAW) is a student-led initiative operating within GHS to help protect you from online threats. Whether you're being harassed online, blackmailed, stalked, bullied, hacked, or just feel unsafe – we're here! Our officers are trained with the skills, equipped, and here for you. Confidentiality is 100% guaranteed and no teachers or staff members of GHS is included and will not be notified of anyhing (T&C applied). But you too have to promise that you will not tattle us to the teachers!
